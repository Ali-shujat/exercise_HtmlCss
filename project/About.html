About us

Circus Eats is a place that reaches beyond the boundaries of culinary tradition, while honoring food, flavour, sustainability and community. We welcome our guests to enjoy the seasonal swedish produce in a warm, social, open-kitchen atmosphere.

We’ll be opening our doors from midday and they’ll be a few surprises in store so come and see Circus in a whole new light, start the afternoon off with the sumptuous menu then as the lights go down get ready for our famous Circus performances. A casual Evening and a show with a difference!
